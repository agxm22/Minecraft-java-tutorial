Createing a new world

Minecraft Java Edition

  

  1. Open the Minecraft Launcher and select Java Edition, then click Play.
  2. Once the game loads, on the main menu, click "Singleplayer".
  3. Click the "Create New World" button.
  4. Give your world a name in the text box at the top.
  5. Choose your Game Mode:
    1. Survival : Gather resources, eat food, survive.
    2. Creative : Unlimited resources, fly, build freely
    3. Hardcore : Survival but with one life (locked on Hard difficulty).
  6. Click "More World Options" to:
    1. Add a seed (a code to generate a specific world).
    2. Choose world type: Default, Superflat, Large Biomes, etc.
    3. Enable or disable cheats.
  7. Once you're ready, click "Create New World".
  8. The game will generate the world, and you‚Äôll spawn in it!

  3. Click the ‚ÄúCreate New‚Äù button, then click ‚ÄúCreate New World‚Äù.
  4. Name your world at the top.

  6. You can adjust settings like:
    1. Difficulty, Seed, World type, and Cheats.
    2. Turn on Education Edition, Coordinates, or Multiplayer settings.

  8. When everything is set, click "Create" at the bottom.
  9. The world will generate and you'll start playing!
